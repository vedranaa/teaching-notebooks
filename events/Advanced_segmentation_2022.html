LECTURE PLAN (morning)

15 min: Intro to QIM, intro to 3DIM (lecture, Anders)
Take-home message: Micro-CT allows imaging the microstructure of tissues and materials in 3D. However, analyzing volumetric CT data may be challenging. QIM supports.
Link: Presentation

Take-home message: If your 3D problem can be formulated as surface detection, you may reduce the complexity of your problem.

Take-home message: If your analysis of fibrous material can be done without segmenting fibres, the stability of your approach can be improved.
